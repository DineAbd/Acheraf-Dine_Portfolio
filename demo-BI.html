Project Description

I recently completed a business intelligence (BI) project for a multinational grocery chain with operations in Canada, Mexico, and the United States. The project aimed to help the company visualize its performance over time and refine its commercial strategy. I handled the entire BI process, from connecting and cleaning diverse source data to constructing a relational data model that enabled the analysis of key business metrics. I used DAX and M languages to create advanced calculations, providing insights into sales trends, customer behavior, and market segmentation. Finally, I designed an interactive report with dynamic filtering and visualizations, giving decision-makers real-time access to key performance indicators (KPIs). The solution equipped the company with the tools to track growth and optimize operations across regions.
